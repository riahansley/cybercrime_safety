A Modern Look Into Digital Risks

Understand how harrassment works and how to stay safer online.

What Is Harrassment?

Harassment in cyberbullying is basically when someone keeps targeting a person online on purpose, trying to annoy, upset, or intimidate them

Why Is Harrassment Dangerous?

  • It doesn't stop easily.
  • It spreads fast.
  • It can hurt someoneâ€™s mental wellbeing.
  • It makes people feel isolated.

Types of Harrassment

  • Repeated insults or threats in messages.
  • Public shaming, posting embarrassing stuff about someone
  • Impersonation, making fake accounts to harass other people.
  • Rumor spreading, nonstop lies about someone.

Common Harrassing Methods

  • Direct message spam or group chats.
  • Hurtful comments on posts.
  • Posting/editing embarrassing images.
  • Tagging the person in hateful content.

How To Stay Safe

  • Block or mute the person.
  • Keep evidence screenshots or recordings.
  • Make accounts private.
  • Report the behavior to a trusted adult.
