A Modern Look Into Digital Risks

Understand how hacking works and how to stay safer online.

What Is Hacking?

Hacking is the act of exploring or breaking into computer systems. It can be used for learning, testing security, or causing harm depending on the intent. Knowing how hacking works helps people protect themselves from digital threats.

Why Is Hacking Dangerous?

  • Stealing personal information
  • Financial damage and scams
  • Identity theft
  • Taking control of devices or accounts

Types of Hackers

  • White Hat: Ethical professionals who test systems safely.
  • Grey Hat: Breaks rules but not always with harmful intent.
  • Black Hat: Criminal hackers who attack systems illegally.

Common Hacking Methods

  • Phishing: Fake messages that steal info.
  • Malware: Harmful programs designed to damage systems.
  • Password Attacks: Guessing or cracking passwords.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating people into sharing information.

How To Stay Safe

Use strong passwords, avoid strange links, update your devices, and stay alert when online.
